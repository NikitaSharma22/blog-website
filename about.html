Hi, my name is Nikita.

I’m 18 and a college student. I think a lot, maybe more than I should, about life, people, and the small everyday things that others might miss.

This is my space to pause and process. I write about the books I love, movies that stay with me, and the strange thoughts that come out of nowhere, from dreams of faraway places to memories of family. Sometimes it’s light and soft. Sometimes it’s heavy and sad. But it’s always honest.

When I’m not writing, I’m usually reading late into the night, doing random coding, or zoning out. I’m still figuring out who I am, but I know I love stories, small kindnesses, and that feeling you get when something finally makes sense.

Thanks for being here. I hope you find something that feels familiar.
